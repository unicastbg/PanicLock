Privacy Policy for Payment Tracker

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Payment Tracker is developed and published by unicastbg. This privacy policy explains how the app handles user data.

Data stored by the app

Payment Tracker stores information that you enter into the app, such as companies, work tasks, payment amounts, payment history, due dates, notes, locations, attachments, reminders, settings, exports, and backups.

This information is stored locally on your device. Payment Tracker does not upload, sell, share, or track your client, task, payment, note, attachment, export, or backup data.

Internet access and sharing

Payment Tracker does not use internet access to transmit your work or client data. The app does not contain ads, advertising trackers, or analytics.

The only way your entered data leaves the app is when you choose to export, back up, save, or share information. In that case, you select the destination, app, folder, or service where the exported file is stored or sent.

Purchases

Google Play handles purchases and payment processing. Payment Tracker does not receive or store your card details, bank details, or other payment credentials. The app may receive purchase status information from Google Play to unlock full access after a purchase.

Permissions

Payment Tracker may request notification permission so it can show task and payment reminders. The app may also use device restart permission to restore scheduled reminders after your device restarts.

Data retention and deletion

Your app data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete records inside the app, clear the app data from Android settings, or uninstall the app. Exported or backup files saved outside the app must be deleted by you from the location where you saved them.

Children

Payment Tracker is not designed for children and is intended for users who track work, clients, tasks, and payments.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated when the app changes or when legal or platform requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page.
